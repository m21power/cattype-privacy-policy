CatType is a typing practice app. It stays on your device, keeps your data on your device, and never phones home. This policy explains exactly what that means — in plain language, no surprises.
── §§ ──
§ 01

Introduction

CatType is a mobile typing practice application designed to help you improve your typing speed and accuracy. By using CatType, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

§ 02

Information We Collect

CatType does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The following data is never gathered:

Name Email address Phone number Location data Contacts Device identifiers
§ 03

Local Data Storage

CatType stores a small amount of data locally on your device only, including:

  • Typing performance metrics (WPM, accuracy)
  • Session history
  • User preferences (e.g. theme settings)

This data:

  • Remains stored locally on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible by the developer

You may delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the application.

§ 04

Internet Usage

📡

CatType is fully offline. It does not send data over the internet, communicate with external servers, or perform any background network requests.

§ 05

Third-Party Services

CatType uses no third-party services of any kind — including:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Tracking SDKs
§ 06

Data Security

Since all data remains locally on your device, you maintain full control over your information at all times. The application does not store or process any data externally.

§ 07

Children's Privacy

CatType does not knowingly collect any personal information from users of any age, including children. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all audiences.

§ 08

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the application or its distribution page, along with a revised effective date.
